About Us

La Salle Beit Hanina School, steeped in history since its establishment in 1876 and it stands as a beacon of education and enlightenment. Today, we embark on a momentous journey with our very first Model United Nations (MUN) conference, a venture fueled by a passionate group of students inspired by the transformative potential of MUNs worldwide.


The inception of this idea was sparked by a group of students who were inspired by the impact of other Model United Nations (MUNs), felt compelled to bring this transformative experience to their own school. Their vision was to recreate the essence of a MUN within the walls of La Salle Beit Hanina School, igniting a spark of intellectual curiosity and global awareness among their peers. With unwavering determination, we pledge to bring forth the very best in this MUN, for we believe in amplifying voices and championing rights. Every perspective deserves a platform, and every voice demands to be heard.

Our objectives stand as guiding stars, illuminating the path toward a deeper understanding of global issues and the honing of critical thinking skills. We aim to fortify diplomatic finesse, cultivating innovative solutions to pressing international challenges. Through meticulous research, eloquent public speaking, riveting debates, and persuasive writing, we forge the leaders of tomorrow, armed with the ability to navigate the complexities of our world. Distinguishing between the mechanisms of the parliament and the United Nations, we equip our delegates with the invaluable ability to discern power struggles from the art of diplomacy. We aspire to broaden international exposure, drawing insights and experiences from delegates in our regions and beyond.


Within our conference, each committee possesses a distinctive focus and purpose, serving as dynamic arenas where ideas collide and innovative solutions come to light. We cater to a diverse range of concerns:

  • United Nations Relief and Works Agency (UNRWA)
  • United Nations Economic and Social Council (ECOSOC)
  • Arab League (AL)
  • North Atlantic Treaty Organisation(NATO)
  • International Court of Justice (ICJ)
  • United Nations Environmental Programme (UNEP).
  • United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)
  • United Nations Security Council (UNSC)
  • World Health Organization (WHO)
  • United Nations First General Assembly (UNGA1),




Join us in this extraordinary journey through the heart of Jerusalem, in the vibrant halls of Beit Hanina. Together, let us stride forward, armed with knowledge, empathy, and a determination to shape a better world.
